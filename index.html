Wordle Bot

Wordle Bot is here to solve a wordle challenge! Choose a 5 letter word you want Wordle Bot to solve. Push the "Start Game" button and Wordle Bot will make its first guess! If the letter is in the correct position click the letter until the box turns green. If the letter is in the word but not in the correct position click the letter until the box turns yellow Push the "Next Guess" Button and it will make another guess. Repeat the steps to tell Wordle Bot if the letters are in the word and in the right place. Wordle Bot will solve your challenge within 10 turns!
